About

Ever since I was a kid excitedly scrubbing through beta footage, I have been obsessed with the process of iteration and ideation that underlies every video game project. I am driven by the creative process and find video games the most compelling medium by which to portray meaningful experiences and powerful stories. I have 5+ years of experience creating video game projects and excel at rapid prototyping.
